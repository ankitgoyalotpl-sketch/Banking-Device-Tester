How to Connect Your GPS Device
Step 1
Connect your GPS device
Plug your GPS8.0 device into an available USB port on your computer.
Step 2
Click "Connect Device" button
After clicking, a popup will appear asking you to select a serial port.
Step 3
Select the correct port
Choose "USB Serial" or "USB Serial CH340" from the list of available ports.
Step 4
Click "Run Device Test"
After connecting successfully, click the Run Device Test button to begin testing your GPS device.

Terms and Conditions & Privacy Policy

By using this GPS8.0 Device Testing tool, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

2. Data Collection and Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. The data we collect is used only for the following purposes:
